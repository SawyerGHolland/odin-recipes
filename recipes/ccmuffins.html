Chocolate Chip Muffins

Description

Saturday morning was made for muffins. After a long week of work there is nothing better than starting off your weekend with some warm chocolate chip muffins. There is something majestic about a warm muffin, saturday cartoons on the tv, and a day of relaxation ahead.

A breakfast that can be enjoyed by everyone in the house is rare. This is one of those meals though, everyone loves muffins. The chocolate chips are a great touch due to the child like wonder of eating sweets for a meal. This is a meal that will not help you on a scale, but will help you with vibes.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 c. milk
  • 1/2 c. vegtable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 c. all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 c. mini semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 1/2 tbsp. white sugar
  • 1 tbsp. brown sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Grease a 12-cup muffin tin, or line with paper cups.
  2. Combine mil, oil, egg in asmall bowl until well blended. Combine flour, 1/2 cup sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl, making a well in the center. Pour milk mixture into well and stir until batter is just combined; fold in chocolate chips.
  3. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 2/3 full. Combine 1 Â½ tablespoons white sugar and 1 tablespoon brown sugar in a small bowl; sprinkle on tops of muffins.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until tops spring back when lightly pressed, about 18 to 20 minutes. Cool in the tin briefly, then transfer to a wire rack. Serve warm or cool completely.
