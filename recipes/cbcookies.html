Creme Brulee Cookies

Description

Have you ever wanted the best Christmas cookies? If so, boy do we the best cookies that you have ever had. The sweetness and richness of creme brulee in cookie form is the best combination ever created. This is a recipe that will bring the whole family together around the table.

We all want to have a recipe that we pass down from generation to generation. This is that recipe! The first time I made this recipe for my family it was all they could talk about. Next year it was all they were asking for. They didn't care if I were there, they just wanted the cookies there. This recipe will have your family worried more about food than you.

Ingredients

For The Cookies

  • 3/4 c. (1 1/2 sticks) butter, softened
  • 1/2 c. packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 c. granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tbsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 2 c. all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp. cornstarch
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp. salt

For the Frosting

  • 1 (8-oz.) block creme cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 c. powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 c. granulated sugar
