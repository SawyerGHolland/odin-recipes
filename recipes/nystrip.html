New York Strip

Description

The best meal in the world is a perfectly cooked steak on a warm summer night. aroma of the grill is the most scentilating thing. Something great happens when the grill is going and there is marinated steak aout to go on.

Picture this, it is golden hour and there is a 12 oz. New York strip steak that has been marinated all day ready to be grilled. The fire is going, kids are playing in the yard with the dogs, and a cold drink in your hands. That is a perfect summer night and it all revolves around the perfect steak.

Ingredients

  • 4 New York strip steaks
  • 1/2 c. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 c.
    • Lowry's all purpose seasoning
  • 1/4 c.
    • Cavenders greek seasoning

Directions

  1. Set the steak in a cooking pan, cover with olive oil followed by equal parts Lowry's and Cavenders. Let the steaks become room tempurature (a couple hours) covered in the seasoning.
  2. Get the grill heated to 450 degrees. Lightly cover the grill gates with oil.
  3. Remove steaks and place on the grill. DO NOT TOUCH steaks for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Flip the steaks and let them cook 4-5 minutes on the other side. If unsure, use a thermometer until the internal temp is 135-140 degrees.
